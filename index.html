InfoSec Community

InfoSec Community is an association at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Una which aims at bolstering the Cybersecurity culture among the college students. The basic motive of this community is to train enthusiasts in different areas of Cybersecurity and to encourage them into participating in CTFs happening in different parts of India and worldwide.

Introduction

We are an agglomeration of highly motivated Security enthusiasts who love to learn and share their knowledge with everyone lured towards Cybersecurity. We conduct weekly community meetings where technocrats are exposed to CTFs in general and how to protect their systems against cyber attacks.

What We Do

  1. Capture The Flag (CTF)
    • We actively participate in CTFs both online as well as on-site.
    • We also organize our own Capture The Flag competitions.
  2. Workshops: We conduct workshops where students are exposed to different areas of Cybersecurity.

Scope

InfoSec Community works on creating a team of Cybersecurity enthusiasts specialising in different areas including:

  1. Binary Exploitation
  2. Reverse Engineering
  3. Forensics
  4. Web Application Security
  5. Cryptography
